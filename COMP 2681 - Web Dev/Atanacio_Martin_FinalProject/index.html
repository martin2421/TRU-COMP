No Sugar. No Oil.

We are El Salvador's top coffee exporters.

Harvested, processed, and roasted here in the tropical heart of Central America.

Here at El Pacto, we are encouraged and committed to becoming a resource positive company. We work hard on eliminating waste, conserve and replenish the freshwater that our beautiful forests provide. Located in Finca Las Brumas, our current estate is in the mountainous area of Cordillera del Balsamo, 1250 meters above sea level, where we grow our notorious, earthy cherries. We take advantage of our country's hilly land alongside its volcanic soil that is rich in nitrogen to continually produce cherries at a large scale all year round. Each fruit is selectively picked by hand in order to provide only the best crops to our processing units.

QUALITY is our top priority. It is our respnsibility to deliver the Salvadoreans with a natural, top-notch coffee. To do so, El Pacto will continue to adopt its tradition of producing natural coffee by exploiting the nutrient-rich soil that our lands provide.
