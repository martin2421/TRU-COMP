EL PACTO

Jesus Hernandez, a man of humble beginnings, yet extremely dedicated, first began growing coffee in El Salvador back in the 1800's. Jesus was so committed and such a fine trader that exceptionally soon, he would convert his little plot of land into a sizeable and prosperous plantation. Needless to say, many Salvadorans were disturbed attributing the man's prosperity to the man's work alone. There must be another side to it. Dedicated Catholics as they were, gossip began that Jesus owed his favorable luck to a pact he had made with some dark power…

200 Years later, his family - the Parduccis - still specialize in coffee production. The family has a good banter looking back at El Pacto's (The Pact) origins. As of now, Aldo Parducci is the manager and administrator at Finca Las Brumas, located in the sloping area of Cordillera del Balsamo, almost 1,250 meters above sea level - perfect conditions for coffee crops!

El Pacto is presently the brand of Las Brumas coffee production. It possesses a new significance to individuals associated with developing it. The fact that the Parduccis have stayed as a coffee developing family all this time is genuinely striking.

The main coffee produced over at Las Brumas is the Pacamara, a Salvadorean varietal brought into the world from intersections among Maragogype and Pacas beans. It is a great balance between a fruity coffee with wonderful sharp acidic flavour and a sweet chocolate finish. What's more, as Pacamara is definitely not an especially safe varietal, managing and pruning are a ceaseless occupation at Las Brumas as well as numerous other nearby farms. The manner in which Aldo's family deals with their plantation and coffee-making procedures is truly a confirmation of their responsibility and assurance to beat difficulties to save the neighborhood climate and further develop cup quality.

Coffee alone is liable for 22% of the whole rural produce in El Salvador. With great motivation, Aldo will continue expanding and growing into a multinational coffee export business. It is a pact that he has made to himself.
